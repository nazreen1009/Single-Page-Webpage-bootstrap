ABOUT THIS SITE

Your New Zealand export business, big or small, is important to New Zealand's
economy. This toolkit was created to help New Zealand business tell an effective
and consistent story overseas. What is the NZ story?

Why is this toolkit important?

Many overseas perceptions of New Zealand relate to our clean environment and
farming expertise. However it isn't widely known that we are innovative,
resourceful, and have a competitive business edge in many other sectors too.
